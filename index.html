Ruobing Zheng

Currently I'm a Research Scientist at Inclusion-AI, Ant Group.

My research interests include Multimodal LLMs, Visual Synthesis, and Interactive AI Systems.

I earned my Ph.D from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I was advised by Prof. Baoping Yan and Prof. Miron Livny. I received my B.E. from Shandong University.

Before late 2024, my work centered on enterprise digital avatar systems. I have since pivoted to multimodal foundation models, with a dedicated focus on post-training for reasoning. My work has been widely used in AQ, Taobao Live, Ant Insurance, Ant-Wealth, etc.
